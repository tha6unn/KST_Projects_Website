FAQ

What's KST projects Do?

KST projects primarily focus on assisting college students with their academic projects, providing tailored solutions to help them succeed in their coursework and beyond.

What types of projects can KST assist with?

KST can assist with a variety of projects, including AI/ML, IoT, web development, mobile app development, CAD design, automation, robotics, and more, tailored to meet the specific requirements of college students.

Do they provide customize projects?

Yes, KST offers customized projects, designed to fit the unique needs and specifications of students, ensuring a personalized and impactful learning experience.

Do KST provide free projects?

Yes, KST provides a few free templates and small projects without UI/UX, along with guidance, to help students get started on their projects.
